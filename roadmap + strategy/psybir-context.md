## 7. Payload CMS vs Firebase – Real Comparison

### Payload CMS Strengths
- Strong content modeling
- CMS-style admin
- Flexible schema
- Self-hosted control
- Works well for pSEO content factories

### Firebase Strengths
- Managed infrastructure
- Authentication and permissions out of the box
- Event-driven architecture
- Easy integration with Google services
- Faster product velocity
- Less DevOps overhead

### Constraints
- Firebase is opinionated (Firestore as primary DB)
- Payload offers more DB flexibility (Mongo/Postgres)
- Firebase does not inherently improve SEO rankings

### Recommendation (10–20 clients)
- Firebase is appropriate for:
  - Client dashboards
  - Lead ingestion
  - Auth
  - Automations
- Payload is appropriate for:
  - Large-scale content management
  - pSEO-heavy editorial systems

Hybrid is possible but increases complexity early.
